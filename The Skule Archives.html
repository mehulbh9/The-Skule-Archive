ABOUT THE CLUB

Welcome! Skulepedia is a resource dedicated to documenting and preserving Skule™'s rich history. It has received contributions from numerous students, past and present (maybe even future!). We welcome all users to add and edit. Disclaimer: This website, Skulepedia, has no formal relation to and is operated independently of the University of Toronto Engineering Society. The content within these pages does not reflect the views of the University of Toronto Engineering Society or the Faculty of Applied Science and Engineering.


The Webmaster maintains the frontend web services that EngSoc provides, from this skule.ca to courses.skule.ca. They often help clubs set up Skule™ websites, and they coordinate with the Computer Systems Administrator to resolve problems which interfere with access to online services or resources. They are the guardian of the Skule™ online domain..
